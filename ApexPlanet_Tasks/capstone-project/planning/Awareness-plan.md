# Awareness Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the strategy to improve user awareness and educate participants based on the phishing simulation results.

## Objectives
- Educate users about phishing risks and social engineering tactics.
- Help users identify common phishing indicators.
- Reduce susceptibility to phishing attacks through awareness.

## Awareness Strategy
- Provide educational material after simulation.
- Explain how the phishing email worked.
- Highlight red flags present in the simulated attack.
- Encourage safe email handling practices.

## Training Topics
- Identifying suspicious senders and domains.
- Recognizing urgent or manipulative language.
- Checking links before clicking.
- Avoiding credential submission on unknown pages.
- Reporting suspicious emails.

## Communication Plan
- Post-simulation awareness message or report.
- Educational walkthrough of phishing lifecycle.
- Security tips and best practices.

## User Guidance
- Verify sender authenticity.
- Inspect URLs carefully.
- Avoid downloading unknown attachments.
- Report suspicious activity immediately.

## Success Criteria
- Increased awareness demonstrated through analysis.
- Reduced interaction rates in future simulations (if repeated).
- Positive engagement with educational material.

## Ethical Considerations
- Maintain respectful and educational tone.
- Avoid blaming or shaming participants.
- Focus on learning and improvement.
